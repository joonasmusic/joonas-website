About

I'm Joonas — an 18-year-old singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist from Köln, building songs out of whatever's in front of me: a street corner, a loop pedal, a half-remembered melody from a trip somewhere new.

Right now I'm in the "In the Making" era — the part before the breakthrough, where the songs are still raw and the world is still the material. New music is on the way, including a bossa nova-leaning track called "Que Quiero Mas," sung in English and Spanish.

I play live regularly, from busking sets to staged shows, and I'm always looking for new rooms, streets, and stages to play.
